About us

GCU racing is a society centred around motor racing, the primary objective of the society is to compete in the Formula Students UK (FSUK) competition. We are currently designing & building our car for the 2023 competition, our first challenger for the Class 1 competition. Our goal for this season is to win the Best Newcomer Award.
